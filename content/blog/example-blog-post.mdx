# 5 Tips for Building Better E-commerce Websites

E-commerce is more competitive than ever. Here are 5 essential tips to help your online store stand out.

## 1. Optimize for Mobile

Over 60% of online shopping happens on mobile devices. Make sure your site is mobile-first.

## 2. Simplify Checkout

Reduce friction in the checkout process. Every extra step costs you sales.

## 3. Use High-Quality Images

Product photos are crucial. Invest in professional photography.

## 4. Implement Search Functionality

Help customers find what they're looking for quickly.

## 5. Build Trust with Reviews

Social proof is powerful. Display customer reviews prominently.

## Conclusion

Follow these tips to create an e-commerce experience that converts.
