# Shopify vs WooCommerce: Which is Better?

Choosing an e-commerce platform is one of the most important decisions for your online business.

## Overview

### Shopify

Shopify is a hosted e-commerce platform that handles everything for you.

### WooCommerce

WooCommerce is a WordPress plugin that gives you complete control.

## Which Should You Choose?

**Choose Shopify if:**

* You want something that "just works"
* You don't want to manage hosting
* You need 24/7 support
* You're not technical

**Choose WooCommerce if:**

* You want full control
* You're comfortable with WordPress
* You want to avoid monthly fees
* You need unlimited customization.

## Final Verdict

Both platforms are excellent. Your choice depends on your specific needs, technical skills, and budget.
